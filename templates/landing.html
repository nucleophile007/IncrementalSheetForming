Toolpath Generation for Incremental Sheet Forming

Welcome to the toolpath generation web app for incremental forming. This application allows users to generate precise toolpaths(contour and spiral) for single point Incremental Forming process, utilizing CAD model with .STEP extension. Along with toolpath, you will also be able to get points and normal vectors. Additionally, you can view your 3D model and simulate the path generation.
